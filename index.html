Welcome to the International Standardized Testing Organization

ISTO provides knowledge-based test on ISO standards with useful data analytics

Elevate your business to the next level

  • Ensure your staff is competent in the ISO standard with an online multiple choice test 

  • Staff who gets awarded the Certificate of Achievement  

  • Personalised A C C U R A T E analysis for each test taker

  • No prerequisite requirements

What do you receive from an ISTO Test

Certificate of Achievement

Qualification: a certificate of achievement upon passing and certificates your understanding of the respective standard. 

Analytics Report

Analysis: an analytics report which measures your level of understanding in the eight (8) A C C U R A T E domains

Ranking 

Star Diagram Explanation: ranks a candidate's performance against the candidate population
