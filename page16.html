ISTO

International Standardization
Testing Organization is a comprehensive testing organisation. Founded in 2001, ISTO aims to provide independent, worldwide tests of understanding of the ISO 9000, 14001, 45001, 22000 and 27000 series. 

If you have any questions, please contact us via email.
