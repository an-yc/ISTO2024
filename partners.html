ISTO Partners

ISTO requires a strong network of partners to deliver high-quality, standardized tests. If you wish to help professionals in your local area acquire ISTO qualifications, you can become an Authorised Test Centre Partner. 

We have two types of partners: Authorised Test Centre Partners and Organisational Partners

  • Authorised Test Centre Partners: a public test centre open to all candidates in locations across the world 
  • Organisational Partners: an in-house centre exclusively for your own organisation's personnel.

Process to become an ISTO Partner

1

Complete the partnership form to express your interest.

2

Undergo an evaluation by ISTO to ensure your centre meets our standards for test security, candidate experience, and professionalism.

3

Sign a partnership agreement outlining your responsibilities and benefits.

Becoming an Authorised Testing Partner:

  • You will be enhancing the knowledge of professionals in your local area or organisation on important ISO management system standards.
  • Delivering standardized ISTO tests helps ensure consistency across the competence of various professionals.
  • It offers an opportunity for business growth by providing a new professional training and certification service.
  • For in-house partners, the candidate analytics reports help identify opportunities in terms of training needs or competence building.

Becoming an Organisational Partner

    Organisations can directly partner with ISTO to offer tests in-house for their employees. The benefits include tailored assessments, robust analytics for identifying continual improvement opportunities, and aligning workforce skills with strategic priorities.

All ISTO tests are conducted online with our Authorised Test Center Partners or Organisational Partners

Organisational Partners can organise ISTO tests at their offices.

Organisational Partner Solution

ISTO Tests are available for the following ISO standards

ISO 9001 Quality management system

ISO 14001 Environmental management system

ISO 45001 Occupational health and safety management system

ISO 22000 Food safety management system

ISO/IEC 27001 Information security management system

ISO 50001 Energy management system
