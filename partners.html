ISTO Partners

ISTO requires a strong network of partners to deliver high-quality, standardized tests. If you wish to help professionals in your local area acquire ISTO qualifications, you can become an Authorized Test Centre Partner. 

We have two types of partners: Authorized Test Centre Partner and Strategic Partner

  • Authorized Test Centre Partner: a public test centre open to all candidates in locations across the world 
  • Strategic Partner: an in-house centre exclusively for your own organisation personnel

Process to become an ISTO Partner

1

Complete the partnership form to express your interest.

2

Undergo an evaluation by ISTO to ensure your centre meets our standards for test security, candidate experience, and professionalism.

3

Sign a partnership agreement outlining your responsibilities and benefits.

Becoming an Authorized Test Centre Partner:

  • You will be enhancing the knowledge of professionals in your local area or organisation on important ISO management system standards.
  • Delivering standardized ISTO tests helps ensure consistency across the competence of various professionals.
  • It offers an opportunity for business growth by providing a new professional training and certification service.
  • For in-house partners, the candidate analytics reports help identify opportunities in terms of training needs or competence building.

Becoming a Strategic Partner:

    Organisations can directly partner with ISTO to offer tests in-house for their employees. The benefits include tailored assessments, robust analytics for identifying continual improvement opportunities, and aligning workforce skills with strategic priorities.
